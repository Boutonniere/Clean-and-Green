What We Are Aiming

Make a Difference After you have developed an awareness of the surroundings, you may start instructing people around you. The symposium serves as a springboard for you to start playing the part of an environmental steward, and it offers online courses and other tools to help you stay on track. Selecting an environmental problem that you feel is most urgent will assure your continuous engagement as a global citizen. There are countless environmental issues, and while they are all significant, it is preferable to start by concentrating on just one of them. You'll quickly see the interconnectedness of all environmental challenges and choose your area of interest. When you've chosen your choice, you tell your neighbors, friends, and family about its significance and urgency.and kin, develop helpful neighborhood initiatives, and identify more causes to support.
