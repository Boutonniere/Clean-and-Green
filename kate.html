How Important does a Clean and Green Environment to Have

Today, having a clean, green atmosphere is essential. Life has been negatively impacted by environmental pollution in the form of contaminated air, water, and land. The soil is poisoned, the water is unusable, and the air is unhealthy, all of which contribute to a wide range of illnesses. In order for the planet to exist, pollution must be stopped and reversed. Only if the basic requirement of clean air and land is met is a healthy existence conceivable. An unpolluted environment and a higher standard of living are implied by a clean and green environment.

Why is it good and green?

Promoting cleanliness is necessary for the sake of hygiene and health. Scavengers are drawn to litter that is laying about, and the unsanitary conditions cause many illnesses. On the other side, green has a clear connection to supporting health. Literally, a green environment is one with lots of trees and other vegetation. By absorbing harmful chemicals and emitting oxygen, trees play a crucial role in air purification. Also, being around flora calms and relaxes people. The tiny insects, animals, and birds that make up the natural environment primarily rely on trees and plants to provide them with food and shelter.

Green, in a metaphorical sense, is being considerate of the environment. A school that replaces all of its systems with environmentally friendly ones, such as electronic communication in place of paper circulars, is said to be a "green school." Recycling is promoted especially among kids, and less electricity is utilized. Power-saving equipment is also used.

How do we maintain a clean, green environment?

By expanding the tree planting program and Green space is a crucial element in maintaining a clean, green environment. Waste management best practices must be promoted and, if necessary, rewarded. Vehicle gas emissions should be monitored, and ecologically friendly fuel alternatives should be promoted. It is important to discourage and penalize deforestation. If necessary, a different region must be prepared for forestation. Plastic and other non-biodegradable materials should be banned from use completely. Individuals need to be made aware of the value of a clean, green environment. Programs that promote awareness are helpful in this. We must put an end to firecrackers and open flames.



Maintaining a clean, green environment is crucial for the continued evolution of life.
