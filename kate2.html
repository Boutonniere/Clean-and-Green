Environmental Protection


Environmental protection is the activity of governments, institutions, and private citizens preserving the environment. Its goals are to preserve natural resources and the current natural environment, as well as to repair harm and reversal trends where practical. The practice of environmental protection involves everyone—individuals, groups, and governments—protecting the environment. Its goals are to protect the environment and the natural resources that already exist, as well as to repair harm and reversal trends where practical.
